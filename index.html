Who We Are

New Bright Ideas is a boutique management consultancy based in Toronto. We specialise in AI strategy, digital transformation, and organisational change for enterprise clients who need more than another framework — they need a spark.

What We Believe

The best organisations are the ones that never stop questioning their own assumptions. We build strategies that challenge orthodoxy, communities that accelerate thinking, and change programmes that actually land.

Our Edge

We combine rigorous management consulting discipline with the creative instincts of artists, community builders, and independent thinkers. We have been building the future of work since before it had a name.

Our Services Start a Conversation

Our Approach

Strategy is only as good as the change it creates.

Most consultancies deliver a slide deck and leave. We stay until the idea takes root. We work at the intersection of rigorous analysis and creative provocation — because organisations that only optimise never lead.

Our engagements are designed to transfer capability, not create dependency. Every programme we deliver leaves the organisation smarter, more adaptive, and more confident in navigating what comes next.

01
Challenge the Frame

We begin every engagement by questioning whether you are solving the right problem. The best insight is often the one nobody wanted to hear.

02
Build for Humans

AI, data, and digital transformation succeed or fail based on people. Our OCM expertise is not an add-on — it is the foundation of every strategy we design.

03
Think in Communities

The most durable change happens when organisations develop the capacity to think differently together. We design for that — through salons, workshops, and embedded programmes.

04
Act with Urgency

The AI revolution is not a future event. Our clients need to be moving now. We deliver fast, structured, and implementable — not endlessly iterated.

Founder & Principal

enitsirhC

Christine is the founder of New Bright Ideas and a management consultant with 20+ years building transformation programmes that actually change how organisations operate. She holds post-graduate credentials from MIT Sloan, London School of Economics, and Schulich School of Business, and is certified in ISO 56000 Innovation Management, PROSCI Change Management, and PMP.

She is also the first Canadian woman to receive a Burning Man art grant for large-scale interactive flame-effect installation — and built a mentorship programme that opened that path to other Canadian artists. She hosts private thought salons across a community of thinkers, makers, and leaders. She presents and organises training through CMC Canada. She is Toronto's first Claude Community Ambassador.

Her guiding principle — "You don't like those ideas? I got others" — is also the operating philosophy of New Bright Ideas.
