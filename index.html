Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 13, 2026

Your privacy matters to us. At Product Works, we believe that trust is earned—and part of that trust is being open about how we handle your data.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it when you visit our website, get in touch with us, or work with our team.

We don’t track more than we need. We don’t share your data unless we have to. And we never sell your information. Our goal is to make your experience simple, secure, and respectful of your privacy from start to finish.

Please take a moment to read through the details below. If anything’s unclear or if you have questions, we’re always here to help—just reach out at privacy@newbrightideas.com.

1. Information we collect

We may collect basic personal information when you:

  • Fill out a contact form
  • Apply for a job
  • Sign up for updates or request a service

This may include your name, email address, company name, and any message you choose to send us.

2. How we use your information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to inquiries
  • Review job applications
  • Improve our services and website
  • Communicate with you when you’ve asked us to

3. Cookies

Our site may use cookies to understand how visitors use it. You can adjust your browser settings to decline cookies if you prefer.

4. Data security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information, but no system is 100% secure. By using our site, you acknowledge this risk.

5. Third-party services

Our website may link to external services (e.g., Cloudflare, social media, analytics tools). We’re not responsible for the privacy practices of those platforms.

6. Your rights

You can contact us at any time to:

  • Request a copy of your personal data
  • Ask us to delete your data
  • Withdraw consent for communications

7. Contact

For questions about this policy or your data, please contact: privacy@newbrightideas.com
